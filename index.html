Here is how pets can help you!

According to the Animal Health Foundation:

  1. Mentally:
    • Pets reduce stress and anxiety.
    • More Physical activity
    • Alleviation of depression.
  2. Physically:
    • Reduced risk of stroke
    • Reduced risk of increasing blood pressure
    • Lower risk of heart attack
  3. Here are more reasons you should get a pet:
    • Pets can improve your mood, spending time with your pets increases levels of oxytocin, aka, the love hormone
    • Pets can improve your social skills, it is so easy to just startup a conversation with someone if you both have pets, setting up playdates, walks in the park, etc..
    • According to a reasearch study cited by Psychology Today, pets can help couples lower their blood pressure, and it can cause them to be happier and have a healthier lifestyle
